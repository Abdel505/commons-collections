/**
30 
 * An IndexedCollection is a Map-like view onto a Collection. It accepts a
31 
 * keyTransformer to define how the keys are converted from the values.
32 
 * <p>
33

34

35

36 
 * </p>
37 
 * <p>
38

40

41
